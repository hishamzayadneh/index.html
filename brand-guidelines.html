Putting knowledge to work

One brand, one voice

This document is the single source of truth for the Abu Dhabi Real Estate Centre brand. It defines our purpose, positioning and tone of voice, then translates them into a precise visual system — the master identity, clear space and minimum sizes, colour palette, typography, photography, iconography and layout grids. Consistent use of these elements reinforces our distinctive character on and offline, from stationery and social media to outdoor advertising and brochures.
